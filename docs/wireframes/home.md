# Home Page Wireframe

## Page goal

Establish Abadis Tejarat Arka's credibility, explain the trading focus quickly, and route users into products, categories, manufacturers, or inquiry.

## Wireframe

### 1. Header

- Logo
- Primary nav: About, Products, Manufacturers, Contact
- Locale switcher
- Primary CTA: Contact / Inquiry

### 2. Hero

- Eyebrow: Abadis Tejarat Arka / ATA positioning
- H1: what the company trades and who it serves
- Supporting paragraph
- Primary CTA: Contact us
- Secondary CTA: Explore products
- Supporting visual or branded illustration area

### 3. Company positioning block

- Short explanation of who ATA serves
- 3 or 4 core value points:
  - sourcing reliability
  - product breadth
  - manufacturer network
  - responsive B2B contact

### 4. Featured categories

- Section heading
- Short intro
- Category cards linking to category pages

### 5. Featured products

- Section heading
- Product cards with:
  - image
  - name
  - category
  - availability tag
  - optional public price
  - view product CTA

### 6. Featured manufacturers

- Section heading
- Manufacturer cards with:
  - logo or initials
  - name
  - short summary
  - related product count or CTA

### 7. Trust / strengths section

- 3 to 5 trust statements
- optional proof points:
  - sourcing expertise
  - manufacturer relationships
  - product coverage
  - responsive inquiry handling

### 8. Inquiry CTA band

- Short persuasive heading
- 1 paragraph
- CTA button to contact page or inquiry form anchor

### 9. Optional FAQ / support block

- 3 to 5 concise questions
- answers focused on sourcing/contact clarity

### 10. Footer

- company blurb
- nav links
- contact details
- locale links
- legal/privacy links when added

## Mobile notes

- hero CTAs stack
- featured cards become single-column
- footer groups collapse into stacked blocks
