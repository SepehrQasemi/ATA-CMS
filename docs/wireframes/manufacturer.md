# Manufacturer Detail Page Wireframe

## Page goal

Show who the producer/brand is and connect that identity to ATA's product offering.

## Wireframe

### 1. Breadcrumbs

- Home
- Manufacturers
- Current manufacturer

### 2. Manufacturer hero

- manufacturer name
- optional logo
- short summary
- origin/country detail if available
- inquiry CTA

### 3. Manufacturer overview

- fuller profile text
- sourcing relevance or positioning

### 4. Related products section

- product cards or list
- each entry shows:
  - product image
  - product name
  - category
  - availability
  - optional public price
  - detail CTA

### 5. Contact / sourcing CTA

- ask ATA about products from this manufacturer

## Mobile notes

- keep hero compact
- bring related products high enough to remain visible without long scrolling
